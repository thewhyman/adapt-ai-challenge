### 1. The Problem (context)
US tariffs are changing at unprecedented speed. The 2025-2026 tariff regime introduced
sweeping changes across 10,000+ HS codes — steel, aluminum, semiconductors, consumer
goods, and agricultural products — with little advance notice. Companies that relied on
manual customs processes were caught off-guard, absorbing costs they didn't model.

The average US importer spends 40+ hours per month manually cross-referencing tariff
schedules. A 2% classification error on a $10M import shipment = $200K in unexpected
duty exposure. Most companies discover this in an audit, not before.

### 2. Gaia Dynamics Solution (core argument)
Gaia's Tariff Audit Engine continuously monitors HS code classifications against the
live USTR tariff schedule. It flags exposure before shipments clear customs, not after.

Three core capabilities:
- **Automated HS code validation** — cross-references your product catalog against
  10,000+ code definitions using LLM-powered semantic matching
- **Tariff exposure modeling** — calculates duty impact across current and projected
  tariff scenarios, including Section 301, Section 232, and IEEPA orders
- **Optimization recommendations** — surfaces FTA utilization gaps, first sale valuation
  opportunities, and bonded warehouse strategies
