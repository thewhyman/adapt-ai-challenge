## Platform Algorithm Rules (applied)

- **LinkedIn:** External links go in first comment, not post body. First 3 lines must hook before "see more." Lead with pain.
- **Reddit (r/supplychain):** Zero product language. Sound like a practitioner sharing a war story, not a vendor.
- **X/Twitter:** Links go in a reply thread tweet, not the main tweet. Hooks must be specific and counterintuitive.
- **Instagram:** Stats in caption. 15-20 hashtags in first comment, not caption.
- **Substack:** Depth is the product. Every paragraph must earn its place.
