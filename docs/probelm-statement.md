# AI Fund Visiting Engineer Builder Challenge

**Venture Idea:** Adapt AI  
**Time:** 48 hours from receipt  
**Submission:** Reply to this email with all deliverables

---

## Context

AI Fund is Andrew Ng's venture studio. We identify AI product concepts, recruit exceptional technical builders as Visiting Engineers, and support them through a 12-week residency to build and validate a product. 75% of residencies result in a new company. VEs may become founding engineer or CEO.

**Adapt AI** is an enterprise content adaptation platform. The core problem: organizations produce high-value knowledge content (training materials, documentation, onboarding guides, SOPs, research) but struggle to make it usable across different audiences, formats, and contexts. A sales enablement deck needs to become a customer-facing one-pager, a technical doc needs a non-technical summary, a recorded training session needs to become searchable reference material. Today this is done manually, inconsistently, and slowly.

Adapt AI uses generative AI to intelligently transform, repurpose, and personalize enterprise content across formats and audiences. Think of it as an AI-powered content adaptation layer that sits on top of an organization's knowledge base and makes every piece of content work harder.

## The Challenge

Build a working prototype that demonstrates the core value proposition of Adapt AI. The prototype is the main event. The PRD and TDD are "show your work" documents. Rough is fine. We care about the quality of your thinking and the speed of your building, not the polish of your writing. Use whatever tools, frameworks, and AI assistants you want. We encourage it.

### Deliverable 1: Working Prototype (The Main Event)

Build a functional prototype that demonstrates intelligent content adaptation. The prototype must:

- Be accessible via a deployed URL or runnable locally with clear setup instructions
- Use modern GenAI capabilities (LLMs, RAG, multi-modal processing)
- Demonstrate at least one complete user workflow end-to-end
- Show evidence of real product thinking, not just a tech demo

**Idea-specific requirements:**
- Accept at least one content input type (document, transcript, slide deck, or video) and produce adapted output for a different audience or format
- Demonstrate audience-aware transformation (e.g., technical to executive, long-form to summary, training material to quick-reference)
- Show some form of content structure understanding beyond simple summarization

**Format:** Deployed URL or screen recording + source code (GitHub link).

### Deliverable 2: PRD (Product Requirements Document)

The PRD you used to scope and guide your build. This is not a writing exercise. We want to see the document that actually informed your decisions. It should cover:

- Who is the user and what is their pain point
- What is your wedge into this market
- What is in the MVP and what you explicitly cut
- Why you sequenced the build the way you did

**Constraints:** Keep it short. 1-2 pages max. Markdown format.

### Deliverable 3: TDD (Technical Design Document)

The TDD you used to plan your architecture and implementation. Again, not a writing exercise. We want to see the technical plan that guided your build. It should cover:

- Architecture decisions and trade-offs you made
- Data models and API design
- AI/ML model selection, agent design, and why
- What you would change with more time

**Constraints:** Keep it short. 1-2 pages max. Markdown format. Diagrams welcome.
